River Dell's Third Annual Hackathon

The event is entirely student-run and organized by the Invention Club and Girls Who Code Club at River Dell High School. Since 2016, Hack River Dell has been among the few premier hackathons exclusively organized by and for high school students on the Major League Hacking (MLH) calendar. Last year’s inaugural 24-hour hackathon brought together close to 100 high school students from all over the tristate area to build some insanely cool projects. A complete list of hacks from last year’s event can be seen on the Devpost site. More photos from the event are posted on the Facebook page.
